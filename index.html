Good to know

Questions, answered.

Does it work with my theme?+

Yes. PingMyStock adds a "notify me" button to your sold-out products through Shopify's app blocks, no code and no theme editing required.

How do you know an email was actually delivered?+

Every send is recorded with its delivery status. You get a live log of who was emailed and when, so "did it send?" is never a guess.

What happens if a send fails?+

You're alerted right away, with the reason it failed, so you can fix it before it costs you the sale. That proactive alert is the whole point.

Do you store my customers' data?+

Waitlist emails belong to you. They're used only to send the restock alerts you set up, never sold or shared.

What does it cost?+

It's free to install and use right now. If paid plans arrive later, early installers keep the best deal.
